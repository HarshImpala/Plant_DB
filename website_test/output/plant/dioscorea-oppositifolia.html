About

Dioscorea oppositifolia is a type of yam (Dioscorea) native to Myanmar (Burma) and to the Indian subcontinent (India, Sri Lanka, Bangladesh).

A Dioscorea oppositifolia egyfajta jamgyökér (Dioscorea), amely Mianmarban (Burma) és az indiai szubkontinensen (India, Srí Lanka, Banglades) őshonos. (machine translated)
